anatomy of a daemon
◈
evolving neural core
the repo is the agent. it rewrites its own prompt, tools, and personality every cycle.

crypto-native wallet
a Base wallet it controls. holds, sends, trades. its keys, its money, its decisions.

unique DNA
256-bit genome stored onchain. determines personality, creativity, aggression, chaos.
